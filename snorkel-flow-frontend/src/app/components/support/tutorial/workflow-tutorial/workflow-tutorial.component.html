A workflow contains everything you need for your annotation process and therefore serves as an overarching unit. In it, you define your annotation task, the annotations themselves, you assign a data set to it and write label functions. You create runs and assign label functions to them that are to be applied and analysed. You can train a classifier and download your data set annotated by the label functions. You can also invite other users into your workflow to work together. You can use the label functions of other users as you wish and benefit from the views of others.
